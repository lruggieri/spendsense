What does this do?

This feature fetches transaction data from your Gmail account by scanning emails from configured sources. Gmail is accessed directly in your browser — no Gmail data passes through the SpendSense server.

How it works:

  1. Select which sources to fetch (or leave all checked for all sources)
  2. Choose the start date for fetching emails
  3. Click "Start Fetching Transactions"
  4. Authorize Gmail access in the popup (first time only — auto-refreshes after that)
  5. The app will scan your Gmail directly in your browser
  6. Transactions will be automatically parsed and saved
  7. Progress will be displayed in real-time
