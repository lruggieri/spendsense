About Categories

Categories help organize your expenses into a hierarchical tree structure. Each transaction is automatically assigned to a category based on rules (regex patterns) or similarity matching. You can create parent-child relationships to group related categories together.

System Categories: The 'all' and 'unknown' categories are protected and cannot be modified.
